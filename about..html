My name is Steven Deprez.
I study graphic design in Ghent, at Luca School of Arts.
It is now my second year, and still my drift to create stuff is getting bigger.
For me it's very important to get in touch with another's work, in every field of art and design. I love to work in a chaotic workplace, getting involved with 
too much content and make myself clear what I can filter. It's imperfect work that makes it so interesting, with a lot of play and fun
between typography & image.
I don't see myself as a designer yet, but I'm trying my best.
